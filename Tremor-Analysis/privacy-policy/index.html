Privacy Policy

Thank you for downloading my app! I, Christopher Lee, am committed to protecting and maintaining your privacy and trust.


Tremor Analysis does NOT collect or store any data. Tremor Analysis uses data from your iPhone's accelerometer to calculate the frequency of movement via a Fast Fourier Transform. I do not store any data in any database.
